# Platform Rules Reference
## LinkedIn + Instagram | Format, Length, and Culture

---

## LINKEDIN

### Format Rules
- **Character limit:** 3,000 characters max. Target: 900–1,300 for standard posts. Up to 2,000 for high-stakes personal stories.
- **First line is everything.** The first 1–2 lines show before "see more." If they don't stop the scroll, nothing else matters.
- **Line breaks:** One idea per line. Max 2–3 sentences before a break. White space is engagement.
- **No paragraph walls.** If you see 4+ lines of unbroken text, split it.
- **Hashtags:** 3–5, at the very end, on their own line. Never mid-post.
- **Links:** LinkedIn suppresses posts with links in the body. If linking, put it in the first comment and reference it in the post ("link in first comment").

### Hook Formulas That Work
- The bold claim: "Most AI adoption programs fail for the same reason."
- The specific number: "I've reviewed 47 Power BI dashboards in the last year. Here's the pattern."
- The honest admission: "I got this completely wrong for two years."
- The gap/mystery: "The thing no one tells you about being a TPM at a university."
- The reframe: "Everyone calls it a soft skill. It saved my last project."

### What Kills LinkedIn Posts
- Starting with "I" (algorithm + psychological — nobody cares about you until they care about the problem)
- Starting with "Today I..." or "I'm excited to..."
- Motivational quotes with no original perspective
- No white space
- A CTA that asks for too much

### LinkedIn Carousel Specs
- File type: PDF (not PowerPoint)
- Recommended slides: 5–10
- Slide 1: Hook — treat it like a post hook, not a title slide
- Last slide: CTA (save, follow, comment, DM)
- Text per slide: 1 idea, 20–40 words max
- No dense text slides — if it needs that many words, it's a blog post, not a carousel

## INSTAGRAM

### Caption Rules
- **Visible before "more" cut:** ~125 characters / 2 lines. First line must function as a standalone hook.
- **Total caption length:** 125–200 words for most posts. Personal story captions can run longer.
- **Tone:** Warmer and more conversational than LinkedIn. Same directness, slightly more personal register.
- **Hashtags:** 8–15. Mix of niche (#enterpriseAI, #tpmlife, #immigrantintech) and broader (#womenintech, #AItools, #careeradvice). Place at end, either on same line or separated by line breaks.
- **Line breaks:** Even more important than LinkedIn. Mobile-first. Short lines.

### Reels — Script Format
- **Length:** 55–65 seconds (not 30, not 90 — the 60-second window performs best for educational content)
- **Hook (0–3 seconds):** A visual + verbal hook. One sentence. Bold claim, question, or surprising stat.
- **Structure:** 3 points max. One per 15 seconds. If you have more than 3 points, it's two reels.
- **Delivery:** Write for how she speaks, not how she writes. Contractions. Short sentences. Pauses.
- **CTA (last 5 seconds):** One action. "Save this." "Tell me in the comments." "Follow for [specific value prop]."
- **On-screen text:** Key phrases only — not a transcript. 5–7 words max per text element.

### Reels — Hook Formats That Work
- Start mid-thought: "Nobody told me that [surprising thing]..."
- Start with a stat: "I spend 4 hours a week on something AI does in 11 minutes."
- Start with a question: "What do you actually do as a program manager?"
- Start with a visual action: open a tool, show a before/after, point to something on screen

### Carousel Specs
- **Slide 1:** Must work as a standalone image — if someone sees only the cover, do they want to swipe?
- **Slide 2–N:** One idea per slide. 1–3 lines of text. Large, readable font.
- **Last slide:** CTA — "Save for later" or "Which of these surprised you? → comment below"
- **Aspect ratio:** 1:1 (square) or 4:5 (portrait) — 4:5 takes up more feed real estate
- **Text on slides:** Should be readable at arm's length on a phone. No dense paragraphs.

### Story Rules
- **Length:** 15 seconds of video or 7 seconds of static before tap-away
- **Text overlays:** Large font, high contrast, readable in 2 seconds
- **Q&A / Poll stickers:** Use for community building and content research — these answers become future posts
- **Link sticker:** Use for driving to LinkedIn posts or external resources

## CROSS-PLATFORM REPURPOSING MATRIX

| Original Format | Repurpose To | What Changes |
|----------------|--------------|--------------|
| LinkedIn personal story | Instagram Reel | Shorten to 60 sec, warmer tone, talking head format |
| LinkedIn framework post | Instagram carousel | Strip to 5 slides, one idea each, visual-first |
| Instagram Reel script | LinkedIn text post | Remove spoken rhythm, add paragraph structure, add professional context |
| LinkedIn carousel (PDF) | Instagram carousel | Redesign for mobile aspect ratio, simplify text |
| Community Q&A post | Both platforms | LinkedIn = longer answer + context; Instagram = shorter, warmer |

### Repurpose Rule:
Never copy-paste across platforms. Adapt for the audience, the format, and the culture of each platform. The core idea stays the same. Everything else is platform-native.
