## COMMUNITY Q&A IS A FORMAT, NOT A PILLAR

Q&A-style content still belongs in the system. It just lives inside
whichever pillar the question touches.

Examples of how this works in practice:
- Someone DMs about breaking into {{YOUR_CAREER_TRACK}} → Q&A post
  under **Pillar {{RELEVANT_PILLAR}}**
- Someone asks about a {{YOUR_DOMAIN_CONCEPT}} → Q&A post under the
  relevant technical pillar
- Someone asks about a process or system you've navigated → Q&A post
  under the relevant lived-experience pillar

The mechanic (answering a real question from a real person) stays. The
pillar slot goes to the topic area that earns you the most strategically.

## HOW TO FILL THIS TEMPLATE IN

For each pillar, answer in this order:

1. **What makes you specifically credible here?** If you can't write
   one sentence, the pillar isn't ready.
2. **What does most content in this space get wrong?** This is your
   differentiator.
3. **What are 4–8 specific post concepts?** Concrete, not categorical.
4. **What stays off-pillar?** Every pillar needs anti-patterns.
5. **What audience value does each slice earn?** Junior, peer, senior.
6. **How does this repurpose to the second platform?** Every pillar
   should have a repurpose path or it's too narrow.

The better these answers, the sharper every post the system produces.
